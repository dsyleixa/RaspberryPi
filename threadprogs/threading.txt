int res;

param.sched_priority = 50
res = pthread_setschedparam(pthread_self(), SCHED_RR, &param);
if (res != 0) {
	fprintf(stderr, "Setschedparam failed, error=%d\n", res);
}

Be careful with giving high real-time priorities for your threads, you may block important system threads from running and make your system unresponsive.
